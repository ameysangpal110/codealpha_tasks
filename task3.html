About Me

Designing for humans,
built for the web

I'm a frontend developer with a passion for building interfaces that feel as good as they look. I believe great design is invisible — it gets out of the way and lets the user do their thing.

My work spans from pixel-perfect UI components to full-stack web apps. I obsess over the details: the right curve on a button, the perfect easing on an animation, the smoothest possible interaction model.

When I'm not coding, I'm sketching UI concepts, reading about typography, or exploring the intersection of art and technology.
